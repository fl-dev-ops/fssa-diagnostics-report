# WhatsApp Feedback: Unique Issue List Grouped By Bucket

This is a deduplicated issue list synthesized from the WhatsApp feedback messages.

## Technical

1. **Speech-to-text misrecognizes what the student said**
   Mentioned repeatedly across feedback. Students said their spoken answers were transcribed incorrectly, sometimes even when they spoke clearly.

2. **Student answers are not captured or submitted reliably**
   Several users said they answered, but the system did not register the answer, or behaved as if they had not answered at all.

3. **Flow skips to the next question before accepting the current answer**
   Multiple students reported that the system moved to the next question without properly taking the current response.

4. **Session gets stuck on “waiting for agent to join” or fails to connect**
   Reported on both mobile and laptop. In these cases, the session was initiated but did not progress into a usable conversation.

5. **Voice recording is unreliable**
   Students reported cases where recording was on, but their speech was still not captured.

6. **Response latency is noticeable**
   Some students explicitly mentioned slow response time, long loading, or “taking time” before their answer appeared or before the next step happened.

7. **Session exits or ends unexpectedly**
   A few users said the interview exited before they completed the question, or the session marked itself finished without giving them a chance to continue.

8. **Audio clarity is poor**
   Some students said Sana’s voice was unclear, too loud, or hard to hear properly.

9. **Mic / device handling is fragile**
   There were issues around mic behavior, and one student specifically mentioned headphone/device switching causing interruption.

10. **Language detection is weak for mixed English/Tamil usage**
   One recurring issue was English speech being rendered in Tamil or otherwise mistranscribed because of poor language detection.

## UI/UX

1. **Students want live subtitles or visible text while speaking**
   This is one of the clearest repeated requests. Students want to see what is being captured before or while it is sent.

2. **Students want stop / pause / delete controls for voice input**
   Several users asked for a way to stop a recording, delete a mistaken answer, or retry before submission.

3. **Exit or end-call action is hard to find**
   At least a few users could not clearly find how to exit or end the session.

4. **User message bubble or typed text is not always visible**
   Some students said what they typed or spoke did not show in the UI, even though the conversation progressed.

5. **Laptop layout could be improved**
   Feedback suggested that the laptop view feels too constrained, card-like, or not full-screen enough.

6. **Tap-to-speak behavior is not intuitive**
   Some feedback suggests the interaction around tapping to speak is not clear enough and can accidentally move the flow forward.

7. **Confusing controls near the mic**
   One student explicitly mentioned not understanding why the keyboard icon was near the microphone.

## Conversational

1. **Sana sometimes gives a different response from what the student said**
   Students reported cases where they said one thing but the agent responded as if they had said something else.

2. **Sana sometimes feels repetitive or fixed**
   A few comments suggest the conversation can feel like a scripted sequence of questions rather than a naturally adaptive exchange.

3. **Sana sometimes continues on the same topic without properly understanding the user**
   This showed up in feedback where students said the system kept talking on the same thread even after their answer changed direction.

4. **Some students found Sana friendly and easy to talk to**
   There is also positive feedback in this bucket. A few students said the agent felt friendly, clear, and approachable.

## Overall Pattern

The strongest pattern is **technical reliability** rather than conversational quality or pure interface design.

The most repeated pain points were:

- transcript accuracy
- answer capture reliability
- skipped turns
- waiting / connection problems
- missing live text visibility
- lack of retry / delete controls
